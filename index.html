Sir Isaac Newton

Sir Isaac Newton was a well-known English physicist who wrote the book Philosophiae Naturalis Principia Mathematica published on 5 July 1687. In the book, Newton described the attraction of natural gravity by the law- Newton's law of motion and this has been the basis of classical mechanics. He is also the founder of differential calculus.
